Behaviour consultations

Behaviour counselling is recommended for dogs displaying serious problems, including aggression, separation related problems, unruly behaviour, over-excitement, attention-seeking, compulsive or stereotypic behaviours, fearfulness and anxiety and multi-dog household issues. 

A behaviour consultation of 1 1/2 - 2 hours at your home with full written report and follow-up is £85 within a 20 mile radius of Cambridge.

Behaviour treatment is carried out on veterinary referral.  If you have not already done so you will be requested to contact your vet before the consultation to ensure there are no medical reasons for the behaviour problem and that behaviour treatment is appropriate for any dogs suffering from medical conditions.

Please contact info@camdogs.co.uk for further information and to request a consultation.
